Project Overview

Green Valley Plots is a premium gated community development spread across 25 acres of pristine land in Shadnagar, offering modern amenities and excellent connectivity to Hyderabad's major business districts.

Strategically located on the growth corridor of Hyderabad, this RERA-approved project offers 150 thoughtfully planned plots ranging from 200 to 500 square yards. The development features wide internal roads, underground drainage, 24/7 water supply, and comprehensive security systems.
